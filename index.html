Notes

Model boundary and thesis note

This app separates the thermodynamic phase boundary from the pump NPSH boundary. A water point may still be liquid when Psuction is only slightly above Psat, but a pump may still be unsafe if the NPSH available is lower than the NPSH required plus the selected margin.

The recommended design interpretation is: use the P–h diagram for phase visualization, then use the NPSH calculation for the engineering decision. All pressure inputs must be absolute pressure.

Property model: Psat is calculated with the IAPWS-IF97 Region 4 saturation equation. Saturated liquid/vapor enthalpies are calculated with IF97 Region 1 and Region 2 up to 350 °C; above 350 °C the chart uses a visual interpolation toward the critical point and marks the result as near-critical approximation. For final thesis validation, compare the output against steam tables or NIST/IAPWS data for the exact operating range.
